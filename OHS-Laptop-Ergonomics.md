# *Research & Learn*

## *What are the risks of using a laptop without an external monitor or keyboard?*

* *Neck strain from looking downward at a low screen.*
* *Rounded shoulders and upper-back pain.*
* *Wrist strain from cramped, elevated typing angles.*
* *Eye strain from small or close screens.*
* *Reduced productivity due to poor posture and discomfort.*


## *What ergonomic equipment can improve posture when working on a laptop?*

* *Laptop stands to raise the screen to eye level.*
* *External keyboards and mice to maintain neutral wrist position.*
* *Ergonomic chairs with lumbar support.*
* *Footrests to stabilise posture.*
* *Monitor arms for adjustable screen height.*
* *Standing desk converters.*


## *What adjustments should be made for a healthier workspace?*

* *Monitor height: top of screen at or slightly below eye level.*
* *Chair position: feet flat, hips slightly above knees, back fully supported.*
* *Desk setup: keyboard and mouse at elbow height with relaxed shoulders.*
* *Screen distance: about an arm’s length away.*
* *Lighting: avoid glare and excessive brightness.*


## *Daily habits that reduce the impact of prolonged laptop use*

* *Take movement breaks every 20–40 minutes.*
* *Do posture checks (straight spine, relaxed shoulders).*
* *Use the 20-20-20 rule for eyes: every 20 minutes, look 20 feet away for 20 seconds.*
* *Stretch wrists, neck, and shoulders regularly.*
* *Stay hydrated to reduce fatigue.*
* *Alternate between sitting and standing if possible.*


# *Reflection*

## *What equipment changes can you make to improve your workspace?*

* *Use an external monitor to raise screen height.*
* *Add a laptop stand to avoid looking down.*
* *Use an external keyboard and mouse for neutral wrist posture.*
* *Adjust your chair height or add lumbar support.*
* *Add a footrest for better leg positioning.*


## *What behavioural changes can improve posture and reduce strain?*

* *Sit upright with your back supported.*
* *Keep shoulders relaxed instead of hunched.*
* *Take short breaks throughout the day.*
* *Adjust screen height instead of bending your neck.*
* *Maintain a neutral wrist position while typing.*
* *Avoid sitting in the same position for too long.*

## *How can you remind yourself to maintain posture and take breaks?*

* *Set scheduled movement reminders using Focus Bear.*
* *Use phone or calendar alerts.*
* *Place a sticky note saying “Sit tall” or “Relax shoulders.”*
* *Use wearable devices with inactivity alerts.*
* *Build posture checks into your daily routine.*
