## Research & Learn

### What AI tools are typically used for your role?
*Chatgpt*

### What are the benefits and risks of using AI in a professional setting?
*AI reduces workload by fetching and summarizing information on the Internet. However, the generated output cannot be guaranteed to be 100% correct. Thus, posing a risk to make a wrongful decision due to incorrect information.*

### What types of information should never be entered into AI tools?
- *any information that can be used to identify an individual. (PII)*
- *company intangible assets, e.g. designs, in-house data.*

### How can you fact-check and validate AI-generated content to ensure accuracy?
- *prompt AI to include the reference in every output generated, and validate accordingly.*
- *Cross checking the generated content by manually searching through the content in a different website or source.*

## Reflection

### When should you use AI for assistance, and when should you rely on your own skills?
*Best to use AI when you want a concept to be explained in an efficient way. Or you simply need to lookup a piece code when you forgot it. AI also comes in useful when dealing with data, identifying pattern in a dataset or generating a quick summary for a handful of sources.*

*But when it comes to design, or involve communicating with people, we should rely on our own skills.*

### How can you avoid over-reliance on AI while still benefiting from it?
*Make sure you understand everything the AI generated, and always ask further questions to the original one, so that you are not blindly, passively receiving everything the AI given to you.*

### What steps will you take to ensure data privacy when using AI tools?
*Double check everything you entered or copy and paste doesn't contain PII.*
